Section A2 Award Applications Now Available

Hello Chapter Presidents and Membership VPs,

Happy 2019! On behalf of this year’s Section A2 staff, applications for this year’s chapter and individual awards are now available. We have streamlined the process, including making forms available on Google Docs / Forms and clarifying criteria on the evaluation of applications.

The most notable change is how Bright Spot Awards will be distributed. We encourage Brothers to submit nominations for distinguished members in the areas of leadership, fellowship, service, and advisory. In addition, we are requesting all chapters submit at most 2 Bright Spot awardees; think about those who, although do not hold leadership positions, make outstanding contributions to your organization.

Brothers qualifying for the Ocho Award should complete this Google Form. You're welcome to provide this link to your chapter body for Brothers to complete on their own; upload photos directly to the form.

All other applications are linked in this Google document along with their descriptions. (Google Drive folder if you need to access them later). All applications are due Saturday, March 31, 2019 by 11:59pm. Make a copy of the application and share them with us: apoa2awards@gmail.com

Awards will be presented at this year's Spring Sectionals at UC Davis. Don't forget to register!

Please let us know if you have any questions:
Find us at next week's' Officer Orientation, email apoa2awards@gmail.com, or reach out to us on Facebook: Andy Chan | Adrian Famularcano

In LFS,
Andy Chan and Adrian Famularcano
